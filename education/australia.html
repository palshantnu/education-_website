Study at ANU

The Australian National University (ANU) offers you small classes and access to world-leading academics – including six Nobel laureates.

Global leaders start here. Join the talented community at ANU

At the Australian National University (ANU), you’ll be a part of a diverse community of leaders solving complex challenges and developing real-world solutions. Our academics are experts in their chosen fields and you’ll gain knowledge and skills that’ll enable you to create meaningful change by challenging ideas, embracing different perspectives and taking part in exciting transformative opportunities. Every time you grow, you’ll give people and our planet hope for the future. ANU will welcome and support you. While you’re earning a qualification among the best in the world, your new home will be a meeting place of ideas that will see you build friendships and networks for life. Our diverse and friendly community will help you to settle into your new home and discover everything that Canberra, Australia’s vibrant and safe capital city, has to offer. Successful futures are built at Australia’s #1 university*.

Bachelor of Arts

This three-year degree will equip you with core critical analysis, writing and oral communication skills. Choose from 50 majors, including politics, languages and history and work in a range of fields including publishing, policy and education.

Bachelor of Commerce

Gain the skills and knowledge required to meet the challenges of a changing business environment. Pursue your interests in one or more areas including finance, marketing, accounting and commercial law

Bachelor of Science

Explore possibilities of science from astronomy to zoology with this three-year degree. You’ll have the flexibility to tailor the program to pursue your interests and will work closely with leading scientists in our state-of-the-art laboratories.

Graduate Certificate of Law

The program will help you understand the role of law in a range of areas such as technology and human security. It provides a non-law graduate or a law graduate from an overseas university an entry qualification for Masters of Laws.

Graduate Diploma of Science

Broaden your knowledge or develop it in your nominated area of specialisation including medicine, psychology or neuroscience. It is also a pathway for candidates without an

PhD in Business and Economics

Pursue your interests in these fields as part of our vibrant and innovative research community. You’ll solve complex challenges facing the world within a specialisation of your choice.

Why study at ANU?

ANU is the leading university in Australia and number 30 in the world. Our graduates are ranked Australia’s #1 university for employability. 1, 2 Informing your learning will be renowned academics, cutting-edge research and opportunities to take part in international student exchange programs and career-driven internships. Our wide range of services will help you thrive in your studies and career and when you finish your degree, we’ll connect you with our enriching alumni community of researchers, innovators and entrepreneurs around the world.
